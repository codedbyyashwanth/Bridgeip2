About Bridgei2p

Technology Powering Advanced Business Communication

About Our Company

Get To Know Us Better

Bridgei2p delivers SMBs, Enterprises, and Contact Centers with turnkey solutions that take into consideration clients' unique needs within a very cost-effective framework by combining the finest of open standards telephony and a suite of Telecoms Management Software.

This blend combines the finest of royalty-free software, thriving open-source communities, and readily available bespoke development, all backed by responsible and professional support services, with commercially programmed management tools. It's both justified and supported, so it's a safe bet for any company.

Our Values

Customer Centricity - Fulfill clients’ needs while making their experience as simple as possible.
Excellence - Keep learning and progressing. Always ask for feedback and strive for better results.
Integrity - Keep our promises, be responsible for our actions.

Where We Started

It all started with our founder Sajid Hasnain & Satyendar Singh’s passion for the power of telecommunication technology and how it can transform voice systems to meet the evolving business needs. In 2016, backed by ten years of telecom industry experience, he started Bridgei2p - and the rest is history.

What Happened Next

Since 2016, Bridgei2p has been helping companies upgrade their business communication systems. Our goals have stood unwavering since then - To free the world from the constraints of traditional communication systems. We started with a small office in Bangalore and are steadily expanding our presence across major cities in India.
